#### **Breakout Trading Strategy**

Identify the breakout bar

    Large-body trend bar

Wait for follow-through

    If the next bar is also a strong trend bar in the same direction, the probability of a successful breakout increases substantially

Entry

    Buy The Close

Breakout Pullback — wait for the first pullback after the breakout to enter

    Tests the breakout point, tests the moving average, minor consolidation
