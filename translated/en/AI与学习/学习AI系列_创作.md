# Learning AI Series: Creation

This is my 7th article on learning AI
=============



AI can generate answers instantly.

What you need is not to memorize everything, but to build an efficient index.

Transform information into transferable knowledge structures.

Your taste determines the upper limit of your work.

If you don't know what's good, you can't create something good.



- Sincerity > Showmanship

- Density > Redundancy



Creation is not about producing objects, but about producing yourself.

Through your work, you extend yourself through time. AI as a prosthesis — treat it as an extension of your mind.



Treat AI as a "big child" or an "alien anthropologist."



Big Child

Rich in knowledge but lacking common sense — it needs you to provide context.

Explain the background in detail and provide specific examples.



Alien Anthropologist

Looking at your field from scratch, asking counter-intuitive questions.

Let AI question your assumptions and find hidden blind spots.

Inefficient Questioning

What is deep learning?



Efficient Questioning

I understand deep learning as multi-layer neural networks, but why is 'depth' more effective than 'breadth'? What's the mechanism behind this?



Division of Labor Between Human and Machine



AI is responsible for: retrieval, organization, first-draft expansion, format optimization.

You are responsible for: asking questions, judging value, final editing, giving meaning. AI is your research assistant and first-draft writer.

You are the ultimate editor-in-chief and philosopher.
------------

Suspend Existing Judgments
------

Put your existing judgments, biases, and definitions in "brackets."

Dictionary definition of a chair: furniture with a backrest for sitting.

Pure experience: when I sit in a chair, my back is supported, I can relax without losing balance, feeling "reliable."
-------------------------------------



By imaginatively changing the features of an object, test which ones are essential.

Remove the backrest — is a chair still a chair?

If not, the backrest is essential.

Dialectical Reversal Questioning
------

How can \[negative/obstacle\] become a condition for \[positive/goal\]?



Question Checklist

How does failure bring me closer to success?

How does forgetting benefit learning?

How do constraints inspire creativity?

Multi-Level Follow-Up Questions
-----

What happened?

Why did it happen?

What structure caused this mechanism?

What essence does this structure reveal?



Case Study: Why do I procrastinate?

Phenomenon: I procrastinate when facing big projects.

Mechanism: I don't know where to start, feeling overwhelmed.

Structure: Lack of task decomposition ability, perfectionist tendency.

Injecting Form: Structure and Logic

Identify "dispensable sentences."

They don't substantially help understanding.

Readers can skip them without affecting comprehension.

Delete them.

Injecting Spirit: Language and Temperament
---------

When writing, imagine you're facing a specific person.



Your self from ten years ago (explain clearly what you didn't understand then)

Your child (use language they can understand)

A specific friend (consider their knowledge background and confusion). Trim redundancy.

Write for 1 hour, then edit for 1 hour.

Edit again the next day.

Every in-depth piece of work is part of your assets.

Over time, network effects emerge among your assets.
------------------

Focus on input and output.

Trust that your inner wisdom will surface when needed.
-----------------

There are no ordinary things in life.

Every sense of "dissonance" is worth recording.



Don't Rush to Assign Meaning

Experience before explanation.

Don't start analyzing in the moment of experience.

Give experiences time to "ferment."



Embrace Confusion

"Not understanding" is more valuable than "understanding."

Confusion is the eve of insight.

Record your confusion, not just your answers.
---------------

The Essence of Creation
-----

Creation is not "producing objects" but "producing yourself."

Work is an extension of memory.

You will forget what you thought about, but your work won't.

The process of creation forces you to turn vague ideas into clarity.

"I don't know what I'm thinking until I write it down."

Work lets your existence extend through time.
--------------

Seeds need time to sprout.

Butterflies need to break out of their cocoons themselves.

Insights need to brew. Leave Space for Questions

When encountering a difficult problem, don't solve it immediately.

Let the question ferment in your subconscious. Accept Being Stuck

When you can't write, don't force yourself.

Go do something else. Trust Your Intuition

Your first intuition is often right.

You don't "have" an essence and then express it.

You "create" your essence "through" expression.



Creation is the purest act of freedom.

It is a way to fight against forgetting and death.



Don't ask "Is this work useful?"

Instead ask "Is this work sincere?"

Fully experience life.

Naturally process and let things settle.

Sincerely create your work.
