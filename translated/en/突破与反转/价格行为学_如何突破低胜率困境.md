# Price Action: How to Break Through the Low Win-Rate Dilemma

## 1. Buy or Sell at Any Time: You Can Potentially Profit 90% of the Time

1. **On 90% of the bars on a chart, you could potentially make money whether you buy or sell at any moment.**

2. Using 81 bars per day as an example, typically only **3 bars** are high-probability signals where you can clearly judge the subsequent move with a **win rate exceeding 60%**.

> This indicates that most of the time, the market is not a clear-cut one-directional move. The vast majority of bars belong to an uncertain zone.

---

## 2. The Golden Rule of High Win-Rate Bars

1. 3/81 is approximately 4%, from which a common rule can be derived:

   * **Only about 10% of bars on a chart have high probability (win rate > 60%)**;
   * **The remaining 90% of bars have a correct judgment rate of only 40-60%**.

2. This means:

   * Whether you use stop orders, limit orders, chase momentum, or pick tops and bottoms, **relying solely on "entry points" makes it difficult to achieve a high win rate**;
   * To escape this naturally low win rate, you must **combine wide stops with scaling in**, letting the market "speak" before reinforcing your position.

---

## 3. The Key Lies in Trade Structure and Management

1. Ultimately, whether choosing stop orders or limit orders, the bull-bear win rate for most price action is between 40-60%.

2. This also gives traders operational flexibility:

   * **Design reasonable long/short structures**, entering and exiting in phases;
   * Combine scaling in with trailing stops to manage overall risk exposure.

3. In markets that seem to have only a 40-60% win rate, **if the structure is well-designed and management is proper, you will most likely (even 90%) achieve profitability**.

> A low win rate is not scary. As long as "positioning + management" outperforms simple guessing, you can still capture the majority of profit opportunities.
